NEBULIZER MASK

Bharat Medical Technology manufacture best in quality Nebulizer Mask. It is made from Medical grade soft PVC material which is non toxic and non irritant.

Soft mask design to provide superior comfort fitting to our Patient.

We provide adjustable electric strap for proper positionig of mask on the nasal area

Our Nebulizer Mask comes in sterile packaging which is individually packed in sterile pouch.

Bulk pack of 50m Pcs Per box.

Features

  • Soft, clear aerosol mask with Anatomical form is ideal for long term use
  • Adjustable elastic strip and integrated nose clip for proper positioning of mask
  • The nebulizer kit just plugs into the standard fitting located at the bottom of the mask
  • Tube length : 210 cm

    • Available Option

  • Adult
  • Pediatric

    • INTENDED USE:

    Nebulizers can be used to deliver many types of medicines. The medicines and moisture help control breathing problems like wheezing and help loosen lung secretions.

    Caution

  • Do not reuse
  • Store in dry place
  • Store away from sun-light
  • T <20°c /68°F
